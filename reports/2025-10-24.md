- The Commission Implementing Decision (EU) 2025/2059 clarifies the applicability of Article 34 of Directive 2014/25/EU specifically for contracts related to certain postal and other services in Estonia.
- This decision introduces new obligations for contracting authorities in Estonia regarding the procurement of these services.
- The scope includes both postal services and additional services that may not have been previously covered under the directive.
- Affected entities include public authorities and service providers involved in the procurement process for these services.
- The decision was notified on 30 September 2025, establishing a timeline for compliance with the new obligations.
