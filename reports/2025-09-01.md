# EUR-Lex Daily Digest — 2025-09-01

## Executive Summary

### Key Items
- [1] The Draft Agreement introduces a framework for enhanced cooperation in financial services between the EU, Andorra, and San Marino.
- [2] EIOPA will provide quarterly updates on financial risks starting June 18, 2025, enhancing transparency and oversight in the financial sector.
- [3] The European Central Bank (ECB) will provide a quarterly update on financial risks starting June 18, 2025.
- [4] The European Securities and Markets Authority (ESMA) will provide quarterly updates on financial risks starting June 18, 2025.
- [5] The European Banking Authority (EBA) will provide quarterly updates on financial risks starting June 18, 2025.

### Briefing (~200 words)

The key themes across the items [1] to [5] highlight a concerted effort to enhance transparency, cooperation, and risk management within the European financial sector. 

Item [1] outlines a framework for improved collaboration in financial services between the EU, Andorra, and San Marino, emphasizing the alignment of regulatory standards and the introduction of new obligations for financial institutions. This agreement aims to facilitate market access and enhance regulatory oversight.

Items [2], [3], [4], and [5] collectively introduce a series of quarterly updates on financial risks from various regulatory bodies, including EIOPA, the ECB, ESMA, and EBA, starting June 18, 2025. These updates are designed to improve transparency and inform stakeholders about market dynamics and financial risks, covering a wide range of factors such as market, credit, and liquidity risks. 

The implications of these initiatives are significant for financial institutions, which will need to adapt their risk assessment frameworks and comply with new reporting requirements. The emphasis on timely reporting and analysis indicates a shift towards more proactive risk management practices across the EU financial landscape, ensuring that stakeholders are better equipped to respond to evolving financial challenges.
